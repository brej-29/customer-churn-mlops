# Paperless Billing Engagement

## Churn Signal
Customer is enrolled in paperless billing. While paperless adoption itself is not directly
a churn driver, customers who are digitally engaged expect a high-quality self-service
experience. Poor portal UX, confusing bills, or missed digital communications create
dissatisfaction that accelerates churn in this segment.

## Retention Tactics

- **Digital-first communication hygiene**: Audit email open rates for paperless customers
  and move unengaged contacts to SMS + push notifications. Customers who haven't opened a
  billing email in 3 months are at higher risk — a short "Are your details up to date?"
  campaign re-establishes the communication channel before it goes completely cold.

- **Interactive bill summary**: Replace PDF bill attachments with an HTML bill summary
  email that includes a usage chart, a loyalty streak counter ("You've been with us for
  18 months — thank you!"), and a single CTA for the next best action. Engagement rates
  are 3× higher than static PDFs.

- **Paperless loyalty badge**: Introduce a small in-portal badge or reward for customers
  who have been paperless for 12 months. Digital-native customers respond well to
  gamification elements and status recognition.
