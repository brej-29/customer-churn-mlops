# New-Customer Onboarding Programme

## Churn Signal
Customer tenure is very short (0–12 months). Churn risk is highest in the first year,
often because the customer has not yet derived enough value from the service to feel
committed. Early intervention dramatically improves 12-month retention.

## Retention Tactics

- **90-day success check-in**: Schedule a proactive outreach call or in-app survey at
  days 30, 60, and 90 to confirm the customer's setup is working and identify any friction
  points before they escalate to a cancellation decision.

- **Feature adoption nudge**: Identify one or two underused features relevant to the
  customer's usage pattern (e.g., streaming add-ons if they already subscribe to TV
  services) and send a short how-to email. Customers who use three or more features churn
  at significantly lower rates.

- **First-year loyalty credit**: Apply a small monthly credit (£2–5) to the bill for the
  first 6 months and communicate it as a "welcome credit." Transparency around the credit
  builds trust and increases perceived fairness of the price.
