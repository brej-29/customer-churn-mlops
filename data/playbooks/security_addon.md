# Security and Support Add-On Upsell

## Churn Signal
Customer does not subscribe to OnlineSecurity or TechSupport. Customers without these
add-ons have fewer touchpoints with the provider and a weaker sense of service dependency.
Adding these services increases switching cost and perceived value.

## Retention Tactics

- **Free trial of OnlineSecurity + TechSupport**: Offer a 30-day free trial of both
  add-ons with no obligation to continue. Customers who actively use either service
  during the trial convert at 40–60 % rates and subsequently churn at lower rates.

- **Bundled security package at a discount**: Present OnlineSecurity, DeviceProtection,
  and TechSupport as a bundled "Peace of Mind" package at 20 % below the sum-of-parts
  price. Anchoring on the bundle price makes individual upsell easier.

- **Educational touchpoint**: Send a short email or SMS highlighting a recent security
  incident in the industry and explaining how OnlineSecurity would have protected them.
  Fear-of-missing-out (FOMO) framing, when done responsibly, increases trial sign-ups by
  15–25 % among customers with internet service.
