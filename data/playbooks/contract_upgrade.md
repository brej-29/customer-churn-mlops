# Contract Upgrade Incentive

## Churn Signal
Customer is on a month-to-month contract. This is the single strongest predictor of churn
in the Telco dataset: customers without a long-term commitment face zero switching cost and
are twice as likely to leave as annual or two-year subscribers.

## Retention Tactics

- **Discounted annual contract offer**: Proactively contact the customer and offer a 12- or
  24-month contract at a 10–20 % discount on their current monthly rate. Emphasise the savings
  versus staying month-to-month and frame it as a loyalty reward, not a lock-in.

- **Bundled upgrade + commitment**: Pair the contract offer with a free service add-on (e.g.,
  one month of TechSupport or OnlineSecurity at no charge) to increase perceived value and
  reduce the price sensitivity objection.

- **Limited-time urgency window**: Set a 30-day window on the offer with a clear expiry date.
  A follow-up email at day 10 (if no response) improves conversion without being intrusive.
