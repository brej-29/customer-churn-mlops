# Price-Sensitivity Retention

## Churn Signal
Customer's monthly charges are high relative to peers with similar service bundles.
Price-sensitive customers are more likely to comparison-shop and switch to a cheaper
competitor, especially when combined with a month-to-month contract.

## Retention Tactics

- **Personalised pricing review**: Audit the customer's current bundle and identify any
  services they pay for but rarely use. Offer a right-sized plan at a lower monthly cost
  and frame it as "we noticed you're not using X — here's a better-value option for you."
  Proactive cost-saving builds trust.

- **Loyalty discount milestone**: Apply an automatic 5–10 % discount at the 6-month, 12-month,
  and 24-month tenure milestones. Communicate the upcoming milestone reward in advance so the
  customer has a reason to stay a little longer to claim it.

- **Competitive price-match pledge**: If the customer mentions a competitor's offer, empower
  frontline agents to match or beat it within defined guardrails (e.g., up to 15 % discount
  for customers with > 6 months tenure). Document the policy so agents apply it consistently.
